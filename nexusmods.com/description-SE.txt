[heading]Features[/heading]
[list]
[*] Require and consume an [i]Empty Potion Bottle[/i] to craft a potion or a poison.
[*] Purchase [i]Empty Potion Bottle[/i] from apothecaries.
[*] Get back an [i]Empty Potion Bottle[/i] when consuming a potion or a poison.
[*] Forward changes from [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/266]Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch[/url].
[*] Support [i]Mortar and Pestle[/i] from [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/667]Campfire[/url] and [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/19924]Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul[/url].
[/list]

[heading]Requirements[/heading]
[list]
[*] [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/164]SSEEdit[/url].
[/list]

[heading]Installation[/heading]
[list]
[*] Use your mod manager of choice, [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6194]Mod Organizer 2[/url] is recommended.
[*] Use [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/164]SSEEdit[/url] to ensure changes applied to merchant chests are preserved, in case another mod is editing the same records: see [url=https://tes5edit.github.io/docs/5-conflict-detection-and-resolution.html]xEdit Conflict Detection and Resolution[/url] and [url=https://www.reddit.com/r/skyrimmods/wiki/patching]Patching[/url] to know more.
[/list]

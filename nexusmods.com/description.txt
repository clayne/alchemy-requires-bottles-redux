[heading]Features[/heading]
[list]
[*] Require and consume an [i]Empty Potion Bottle[/i] to craft a potion or a poison.
[*] Purchase [i]Empty Potion Bottles[/i] from apothecaries.
[*] Get back an [i]Empty Potion Bottle[/i] when consuming a potion or a poison.
[*] Forward changes from [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/71214]Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch[/url].
[*] Support [i]Mortar and Pestle[/i] from [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/64798]Campfire[/url] and [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/69306]Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul[/url].
[/list]

[heading]Requirements[/heading]
[list]
[*] [url=https://store.steampowered.com/app/211720]Dawnguard[/url]
[*] [url=https://store.steampowered.com/app/226880]Dragonborn[/url]
[*] [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/25859]TES5Edit[/url]
[/list]

[heading]Installation[/heading]
[list]
[*] Use your mod manager of choice, [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6194]Mod Organizer 2[/url] is recommended.
[*] Use [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrim/mods/25859]TES5Edit[/url] to ensure changes applied to merchant chests are preserved, in case another mod is editing the same records: see [url=https://tes5edit.github.io/docs/5-conflict-detection-and-resolution.html]xEdit Conflict Detection and Resolution[/url] and [url=https://www.reddit.com/r/skyrimmods/wiki/patching]Patching[/url] to know more.
[/list]
